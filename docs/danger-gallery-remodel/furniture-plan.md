## 1. Style Summary

Per the AI design images (`lounge.png`, `installation-room.png`, `moodboard.png`): soft modern monochrome "Studio Lounge Vision" — white/cream/ivory boucle and plush upholstery on heavily curved silhouettes (crescent sofas, barrel/swivel chairs, pouf ottomans), light cream rugs, small round black + antique-gold drink tables, BLACK bar-height bistro tables with black stools by the windows/bar wall, white walls + sheer curtains, black exposed-joist ceiling, warm 2700K lighting, abundant plants.

Sourcing strategy: anchor the set at **Living Spaces** (the Abby / Formosa curved-white families keep whites consistent and ship free or cheap), fill black accents at **IKEA** (New Haven CT store, ~40 min from Stamford — pickup possible), one gold accent at **Target**.

### Assumptions
- Room dimensions unknown; plan uses zone logic + clearance rules, not a measured floor plan.
- **Bar-height (40–42" tables, ~29" stools) chosen over counter-height** — the images clearly show tall bistro tables with long-leg stools.
- Delivery: Living Spaces items ordered together (most flagged "FREE Shipping Eligible"; $150 consolidated delivery estimated to be safe). IKEA estimated $99 large-item delivery, or $0 if picked up at IKEA New Haven with a truck/van. Target ships free over $35.
- Assembly is light (IKEA tables/stools; Living Spaces seating arrives assembled or near-assembled). No pro assembly budgeted.
- Rugs, curtains, lighting, and plants are **out of scope** for this budget (furniture only).
- Prices verified online the week of 2026-07-04 and will drift; sale prices flagged.

## 2. Placement Plan by Zone

Clearance rules used throughout: **~18" sofa-to-coffee-table**, **30–36" main walkways**, ~24" between adjacent seat arms, 3–4 ft breathing room around gallery walls for viewing.

### Zone A — Main Lounge (per `lounge.png`, left/window wall)
- 1× Formosa 100" curved sofa facing into the room, back to the sheer-curtained windows.
- 2× Abby swivel barrel chairs opposite, angled inward to close the conversation circle.
- KRAGSTA 35⅜" black round coffee table centered, 18" from sofa edge and chair fronts.
- 1× GLADOM black tray table between the barrel chairs for drinks.
- Sits on the large cream rug; leave ≥36" walkway between chair backs and the gallery circulation path.

### Zone B — Secondary / Installation Lounge (per `installation-room.png`)
- 1× Formosa 100" curved sofa against the white wall.
- 2× Abby curved boucle settees (68") flanking at soft angles, forming a horseshoe.
- 2× Abby swivel barrel chairs completing the circle.
- 2× Veranda boucle poufs floated inside the horseshoe as flex seating/footrests.
- 1× Manila gold drum table + 1× GLADOM black tray table as staggered drink tables between seats (matches the gold martini tables in the render).
- Keep the center open (projection/installation sightlines); poufs slide out when the room is used for viewings.

### Zone C — High-Top Bistro Zone (per `lounge.png` right side, near bar wall/windows)
- 2× NORDVIKEN black bar tables (55" × 31.5", 41⅜" H), each with 3–4 DALFRED black stools (8 total).
- Set tables ~48" apart so both can seat people back-to-back with a 30"+ pass-through.
- Stools tuck under; DALFRED height adjusts 24¾–29⅛" so they also work if a counter-height ledge is added later.

## 6. Risks & Notes for the Owner

1. **NORDVIKEN bar table is at a 40%-off sale price ($239.99)** — order soon; at regular price (~$399) the pair adds ~$320 (buffer absorbs it).
2. **Living Spaces has no CT showroom** (stock shown in CA/AZ/TX/OK/KS/UT/CO/GA) — everything ships; expect 2–4 week windows (site showed "Get it Jul 18 – Aug 01" on some items). No sit-test before buying; check return/restock terms on the two sofas especially.
3. **Formosa sofa is smooth ivory poly, not boucle** — texture contrast with the Abby boucle pieces is actually true to `lounge.png` (smooth big sofa + boucle chairs), but if full boucle is wanted, the Misty 90" via TOV/Coleman (~$1,399) needs availability confirmation first.
4. **White boucle + gallery foot traffic + wine = risk.** Budget buffer (~$1,500 post-tax) could go to scotchgarding/performance-fabric treatment and 2 spare throw covers.
5. **Delivery estimates are mine, not quoted** ($150 LS + $99 IKEA). IKEA New Haven pickup with a rented van kills the $99. LS may come in $0–$169 depending on cart composition at checkout.
6. Amazon/Walmart/Home Depot/World Market/Wayfair all block automated price checks — several attractive cheaper options (Homall $60-ish bar tables, WM Dunsley coffee table, HD Julia loveseat) are listed above unpriced; a 10-minute manual browser pass could shave another $300–600.
7. Poufs' Veranda $200 price came from a Living Spaces category page without dimensions — confirm diameter (~20–24" wanted) in cart.
8. Everything here is furniture only — rug, sheers, lighting, and plants from the renders are not in this budget.
