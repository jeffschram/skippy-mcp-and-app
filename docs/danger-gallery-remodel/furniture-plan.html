Risks & Notes

  • NORDVIKEN is 40% off ($239.99) — order soon; regular price adds ~$320 for the pair (buffer absorbs it).
  • Living Spaces has no CT showroom — no sit-test, 2–4 week ship windows; check return/restock terms on the two sofas especially.
  • Formosa is smooth ivory, not boucle — texture contrast is true to the renders, but a full-boucle alternative (Misty 90″ via TOV, ~$1,399) needs availability confirmation.
  • White boucle + gallery wine traffic — consider spending buffer on fabric protection and spare throw covers.
  • Delivery figures are estimates, not quotes — IKEA New Haven pickup with a van eliminates $99; Living Spaces may quote $0–$169 at checkout.
  • Amazon/Walmart/Home Depot/World Market/Wayfair blocked automated price checks — a 10-minute manual browser pass over the flagged items in the research list could shave another $300–600.
  • Confirm the Veranda pouf diameter (~20–24″ wanted) in cart — category page lacked dimensions.
  • Furniture only: rug, sheers, lighting, and plants from the renders are not in this budget.
